Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

ReplyAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension and website handle your information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

ReplyAI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

— We do not store your email content.
— We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information.
— We do not track your browsing activity.
— We do not use cookies or analytics.

2. How the Extension Works

When you use ReplyAI, the text of the email you are viewing in Gmail is temporarily sent to our server solely to generate a professional reply. This text is processed in real time and is never stored or logged on our servers.

3. Third-Party AI Processing

To generate email replies, we use Groq's AI API. The email text is sent to Groq's servers for processing. Groq's privacy policy applies to this processing. We do not share any additional personal information with Groq.

4. Permissions Used

The extension uses the following Chrome permissions:

— activeTab: To read the email content from the currently active Gmail tab.
— scripting: To extract email text from the Gmail page.
— host permission (mail.google.com): To access Gmail and read email content for reply generation.

These permissions are used solely to provide the core functionality of the extension.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain any user data. All processing is done in real time and no information is stored after your reply is generated.

6. Children's Privacy

ReplyAI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@replayai.vercel.app
